About Us

🎯

Our Vision

To be a leading institution that empowers every student with knowledge, values, and skills for a rapidly changing world. We envision graduates who are confident, compassionate, and ready to make a positive impact in their communities.

🚀

Our Mission

To provide a nurturing and inclusive learning environment where academic excellence meets character development. We are committed to delivering innovative, student-centered education supported by experienced educators and modern facilities.

📚

Our History

Founded over two decades ago, SRME School began as a small community institution with a handful of dedicated teachers and a vision for quality education. Today, we have grown into a thriving school serving hundreds of students across all grade levels.

Admissions

Academics

A strong foundation for every stage of learning.

Pre-Primary

Ages 3–5 | Nursery to KG

Our early childhood program focuses on play-based learning, creativity, and foundational literacy and numeracy skills in a safe, nurturing environment.

Primary

Ages 6–10 | Grade 1–5

The primary curriculum builds core competencies in English, Mathematics, Science, and Social Studies, with an emphasis on critical thinking and collaborative learning.

Secondary

Ages 11–15 | Grade 6–10

Secondary students engage with a comprehensive curriculum preparing them for board examinations, with dedicated support in STEM, languages, and extracurricular activities.
